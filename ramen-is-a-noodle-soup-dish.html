Introduction:

Ramen, a beloved Japanese comfort dish, has evolved from humble beginnings to a global sensation. While instant ramen is a convenient option, the true magic of this iconic bowl lies in crafting it from scratch at home. Ingredients for Ramen Noodles:

    All-Purpose Flour:

  • The base for the noodle dough, providing the desired texture and elasticity.

    Baking Soda (Optional):

  • Enhances the alkalinity of the noodles, creating a distinct ramen texture.

    Water:

  • Binds the ingredients and forms a smooth, workable dough.

Equipment:

    Mixing Bowl:

  • For combining and kneading the noodle dough.

    Rolling Pin:

  • To flatten and roll out the noodle dough.

    Ramen Noodle Cutter or Knife:

  • For cutting the rolled-out dough into noodle strips.

    Large Pot:

  • For boiling the ramen noodles and simmering the broth.
